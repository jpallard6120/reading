# Artemis

## Verdict

Fun, but a clear step below Project Hail Mary and The Martian.

## What worked

- Fun and readable

## What did not

- The more complex social setting did not play to Weir's strengths as well as his solo adventures

## Reader comments

> Finished project hail mary, then just went through the martian and artemis. PHM is clearly S-tier, The Martian was also great, Artemis was fun but a step below those two. I feel like Weir is amazing for solo adventures, but a bit less for complex social structures building.

## Recommendation implications

### Recommend more of

- Weir-style problem-solving when it remains the center of the book

### Avoid assuming

- A larger social canvas improves a competence-driven adventure
- Enjoying one author's best work means all of that author's settings fit equally well
