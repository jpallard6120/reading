# The Martian

## Verdict

Great. It reinforced the view that Andy Weir is at his best with solo adventures.

## What worked

- Solo survival structure
- Concrete problem-solving
- Weir's competence-driven storytelling

## What did not

- No book-specific objection was recorded

## Reader comments

> Finished project hail mary, then just went through the martian and artemis. PHM is clearly S-tier, The Martian was also great, Artemis was fun but a step below those two. I feel like Weir is amazing for solo adventures, but a bit less for complex social structures building.

## Recommendation implications

### Recommend more of

- Solo adventures
- Competent protagonists solving concrete problems
- Fast, readable technical fiction

### Do not assume

- A detailed social world is necessary when the central survival story is strong
