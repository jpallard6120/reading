# We Are Legion (We Are Bob)

## Verdict

6/10, fast-food fiction. Readable enough to finish, but shallow, cringey, and ultimately unsatisfying. The verdict formed at the halfway point and did not change by the end.

## What worked

- Sufficient momentum to keep reading despite the objections

## What did not

- An obnoxious manchild protagonist placed in implausibly broad authority
- Shallow, dated reference humor
- No meaningful character development
- A standard “space explorer 101” story
- The empty, bloated feeling of fast food

## Reader comments

> Ok, halfway through we are bob... Meh, 6/10. Obnoxious manchild as defacto ruler of earth is hard not to cringe at. Jokes are shallow (All your base are belong to us, are you kidding?), character development is nil, and the story is standard "space explorer 101". We're in fast food territory. I'll finish it because I can't help it, but I'll feel bloated as hell when I'm done.

## Recommendation implications

### Recommend more of

- Competent irreverence inside a serious world
- Humor that reveals character
- Page-turners with adult texture and consequence

### Avoid

- Nerd-reference humor
- Manchild protagonists
- Velocity without depth
- Treating “funny space adventure” as a sufficient match by itself
