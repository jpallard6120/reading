# Diaspora

## Verdict

Hard no. Beautifully written, but the extensive vocabulary made reading feel like work rather than fun. Stopped after about 50 pages.

## What worked

- Beautiful writing

## What did not

- Excessive vocabulary
- Too much cognitive effort for recreational reading
- Felt like a brain workout rather than fun

## Reader comments

> Ok, diaspora, hard no. Vocabulary was way too extensive, it's honestly beautifully written but it felt like work. I want to read for fun, not for a brain workout. I stopped after 50 pages or so.

## Recommendation implications

### Recommend more of

- Clear, accessible prose
- Books that remain fun while exploring difficult ideas

### Avoid

- Vocabulary cliffs
- Fiction that feels like assigned work
- Assuming beautiful prose compensates for high reading friction
