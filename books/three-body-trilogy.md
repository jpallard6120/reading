# Remembrance of Earth's Past (Three-Body trilogy)

## Verdict

Really liked it. A genuine page-turner, with only minor predictability in places.

## What worked

- Strong narrative momentum
- Large-scale science-fiction ideas
- Compelling enough to read the whole trilogy

## What did not

- Some developments felt predictable, but not enough to matter much
